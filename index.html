Revolutionizing Automotive Industry

Dartodi Motors Limited is Zambia's first automotive startup company that started as a JETS project in 2022. We're dedicated to solving the most pressing challenges in the automotive industry: air pollution, long charging times, and short ranges for electric vehicles.

Our revolutionary approach uses smart Hydrogen Production Technology known as CIG (Clean Intelligent Generation), invented by our CEO/Founder Allan Siazyoombo in 2023. This groundbreaking technology reduces hydrogen production costs by 70% while being 50% more efficient than any other known hydrogen production method.

By utilizing renewable solar energy to produce hydrogen, we're not just reducing costs - we're creating a completely sustainable transportation solution that eliminates air pollution.
